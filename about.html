Our Story

Born from a
Porch Swing
and a Big Idea

Southern Bitch started because we couldn't find clothes that matched how we actually lived — bold enough to mean it, soft enough to feel it. We wanted the ease of a garden dress and the backbone of something that lasts. So we made it ourselves.

"We're not interested in fashion that asks you to shrink. We make clothes for women who take up space — gracefully, unapologetically, and always in something beautiful."
— Founder, Southern Bitch

What We Stand For

Three things guide every piece we make, every decision we take.

✦

Comfort Without Compromise

Beautiful clothes shouldn't hurt. Every silhouette is designed for movement, every fabric chosen for feel. If it isn't comfortable, it doesn't make the cut.

✿

Inclusive by Design

We size from XS to 3X because great style isn't a size. Our fits are tested across the full range — not graded up from a single sample.

◈

Thoughtfully Made

We work with small-batch mills, choose natural fibers where we can, and take our time. We'd rather do fewer things right than more things fast.

The Origin

Two Women, One Long Summer, Zero Compromises

It started with a shared frustration: the clothes we loved were either too precious to live in or too casual to feel like ourselves. We wanted both — the smocked bodice and the horseback ride, the linen dress and the lake day.

We spent a summer sketching, sampling, and being brutally honest with each other. The result was a small first run of twelve pieces. They sold out in a weekend. We've been building ever since.
