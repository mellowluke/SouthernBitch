Our Story

Tired of the
Markup.
Done with the Mediocre.

Two women working in the fashion industry for years got tired of all the subpar quality clothing with ridiculously high margins. We're here to make a quality piece of clothing that does not kill your bank account.

"Great clothes shouldn't cost a fortune. We know because we spent years on the inside watching it happen — and we decided to do something about it."
— Founders, Southern B*tch

What We Stand For

Three things guide every piece we make, every decision we take.

✦

Comfort Without Compromise

Beautiful clothes shouldn't hurt. Every silhouette is designed for movement, every fabric chosen for feel. If it isn't comfortable, it doesn't make the cut.

✿

Inclusive by Design

We size from XS to 3X because great style isn't a size. Our fits are tested across the full range — not graded up from a single sample.

◈

Thoughtfully Made

We work with small-batch mills, choose natural fibers where we can, and take our time. We'd rather do fewer things right than more things fast.

The Origin

Two Women, Years of Experience, One Breaking Point

Between the two of us, we've worked every corner of the fashion industry — production, buying, wholesale, design. We saw firsthand how a $20 garment ends up on a rack for $200, and the quality still isn't there.

We started Southern B*tch because we were done making other people rich off bad product. Every piece we make is something we'd actually wear — built to last, priced like it should be.

The Women Behind It

Elizabeth Pignataro

Sarah Shiner
